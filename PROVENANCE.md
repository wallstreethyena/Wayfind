# Wayfind - Provenance Record

This document is a contemporaneous business record of the origin and
ownership of the Wayfind application ("the Work").

Creation. The Work, a consumer location-discovery web application, was
conceived and developed beginning in 2026 and reached production release
(wayfind-xi.vercel.app) prior to July 4, 2026, as evidenced by the dated
version history in CHANGELOG.md, the commit history of this repository, and
the deployment history of the associated Vercel project.

Authorship and resources. The Work was created solely by its founder as a
personal project, on personally owned equipment, on personal time, using no
resources, facilities, funds, confidential information, or trade secrets of
any employer, past or present. The Work is unrelated to the business of
banking or financial services.

Ownership. All right, title, and interest in the Work, including all source
code, the Wayfind brand and slogan, curated content, and related assets, is
held by the founder personally and is to be assigned to Wayfind LLC upon its
formation, with the assignment effective as of the dates of creation.

Prior invention status. The Work constitutes a pre-existing invention and
prior work product relative to any employment commencing after the dates
above, and is expressly reserved from any assignment of inventions arising
from such employment.

This record is maintained in the repository and updated only by appending
dated entries below.
